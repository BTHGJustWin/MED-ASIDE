  • Secure online portal access for real-time account monitoring and document retrieval
  • Dedicated caseworker assigned to every account for personalized service

Lien Resolution

Navigate the complex world of Medicare and Medicaid liens with confidence. Our lien resolution team identifies, verifies, negotiates, and resolves all government and private health insurance liens, clearing the path for a clean settlement closure.

Unresolved liens can delay settlements, expose parties to double-damages liability, and create long-term compliance risks. We eliminate those risks through meticulous research, aggressive negotiation, and documented resolution of every outstanding claim.

  • Medicare conditional payment identification, dispute, and resolution
  • Medicaid lien negotiation and state agency coordination
  • ERISA and private health plan lien resolution leveraging federal preemption analysis
  • Comprehensive audit trail and documentation for legal defensibility
  • Final lien clearance letters and proof-of-resolution packages

Settlement Consulting

Strategic guidance from the earliest stages of settlement planning through final resolution. Our consultants work alongside attorneys, adjusters, and mediators to structure settlements that maximize value for claimants while satisfying all Medicare Secondary Payer obligations.

We bring deep expertise in MSA allocation methodology, future medical cost projection, and benefit preservation strategies that can save claimants thousands of dollars while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

  • Medicare conditional payment (MCP) analysis and exposure assessment

  • Pre-settlement planning and CMS submission strategy development

Trust Services

Specialized trust administration for settlement recipients who require structured asset protection. We establish and manage Special Needs Trusts, Qualified Settlement Funds, and pooled trusts that safeguard public benefit eligibility while providing for the claimant's supplemental needs.

Our trust services team coordinates with estate planning attorneys, guardians, and public benefit agencies to ensure every trust instrument is properly drafted, funded, and administered in accordance with applicable federal and state law.

  • Special Needs Trust (SNT) establishment and ongoing administration
  • Qualified Settlement Fund (QSF) management and tax compliance
  • Pooled trust enrollment and disbursement coordination
  • Estate planning coordination to protect long-term beneficiary interests
  • Court accounting, annual reporting, and fiduciary compliance

Liability Settlements

Tailored Medicare Set-Aside solutions for liability and no-fault settlements. Liability MSAs (LMSAs) present unique challenges compared to workers' compensation MSAs, and our team brings specialized expertise in this evolving area of Medicare Secondary Payer compliance.

From initial LMSA development through CMS submission strategy and post-settlement administration, we provide end-to-end support that protects both claimants and settling parties from future Medicare recovery actions.

  • Liability MSA (LMSA) development and allocation analysis
  • Future medical cost projections specific to liability and no-fault claims
  • Structured settlement integration and annuity funding coordination
  • MMSEA Section 111 reporting compliance and data management
  • Risk mitigation strategies for defendants, insurers, and self-insured entities
