Our Story

Founded on a Simple Premise

Medicare Set-Aside administration shouldn't be a black box. For too long, injured parties have been left to navigate the complex maze of Medicare compliance, trust administration, and financial planning on their own — often falling prey to inefficiencies and impersonal service from large, bureaucratic institutions.

MED ASIDE was created to change that. By combining high-level Medicare compliance expertise with a client-first approach, we ensure that every settlement is structured to provide maximum benefit and security while meeting all CMS requirements.

Our team is composed of seasoned compliance specialists, financial planners, and Medicare experts who work in concert to protect your future. We don't just administer funds — we build relationships that last the lifetime of your settlement.
